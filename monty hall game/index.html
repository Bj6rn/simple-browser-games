Rules

The goal of this game is to choose the one door, which has the car behind. In the first step, the player needs to choose one door. Then the game will open one of the other doors with a goat behind it. Now the player needs to decide wether he will stay with his first decision or if he wants to take the other door. Disclaimer: There's nothing to win for real.
